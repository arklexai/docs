The Settings page manages your personal profile and your organization's team configuration, usage, and billing. Navigate to **Settings** in the sidebar.

---

## Profile

The Profile tab lets you update your account details.

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Display name | Your name as it appears in evaluation results and shared views |
| Email | Your account email address |
| Password | Change your password (requires entering the current password) |

---

## Members

The Members tab shows everyone in your organization and lets admins manage access.

### Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Admin** | Full access to all features including member management, billing, and all team data |
| **Member** | Access to agents, scenarios, simulations, evaluations, knowledge, and metrics. Cannot manage members or billing. |

### Inviting teammates

Click **Invite Member** and enter the email address. An invitation email is sent with a link to join the organization. Invited users who do not yet have an Arkdock account will be prompted to create one when they accept.

Pending invites are shown in the members list with a "Pending" status until the recipient accepts.

### Changing a member's role

Admins can change a member's role from the member row. Click the role dropdown and select the new role.

### Removing a member

Admins can remove a member using the kebab menu on their row. Removed members lose access to the organization immediately. Their historical contributions (evaluations created, annotations added) remain in the platform.

## Usage

The Usage tab shows your organization's consumption metrics for the current billing period:

- Number of simulations run
- Total conversations generated
- Number of evaluations completed
- API calls made

Usage is refreshed periodically. Contact support if you believe there is a discrepancy.

## Billing

The Billing tab shows your current plan, live token usage for the billing period, and overage controls.

### Plans

| Plan | Price | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0/month | Every new organization starts here |
| **Pro** | $299/month | Teams running production workloads |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom scale, compliance, or on-prem requirements |

**Free plan includes:**
- 700K input tokens / billing period
- 100K output tokens / billing period
- 5K knowledge tokens (lifetime hard limit)
- Operations pause at the limit — no overage charges
- Up to 2 concurrent operations

**Pro plan includes:**
- 14M input tokens / billing period
- 2M output tokens / billing period
- 100K knowledge tokens (lifetime hard limit)
- Input overage: $0.01 per 1K tokens
- Output overage: $0.05 per 1K tokens
- $2,000/month default overage ceiling
- Custom spending cap

**Enterprise plan includes:**
- Custom token allowances and overage rates
- Bring your own LLM model/provider
- On-prem deployment option
- Dedicated CSM and SLA
- SSO / SAML

### Token usage

The Billing tab shows three token meters for the current billing period:

| Dimension | Resets | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Input tokens | Each billing period | Tokens sent to your agent and the LLM judge |
| Output tokens | Each billing period | Tokens returned by your agent and the LLM judge |
| Knowledge tokens | Never (lifetime hard limit) | Tokens consumed indexing documents and websites |

Each bar turns amber at 80% and red at 100%.

### Overage and spending cap

On the Free plan, operations pause when any token limit is reached. No overage is charged.

On the Pro plan, operations continue past the included allowance and overage is billed at the per-token rates above. A default ceiling of $2,000/month caps total overage. Use the **Spending Cap** control to set a lower limit and prevent unexpected charges.

### Upgrading and managing

Click **Upgrade to Pro** to start the checkout flow (opens the Stripe payment page). Click **Manage Billing** to update your payment method, download invoices, or cancel your subscription (opens the Stripe billing portal).

To cancel a Pro subscription, use the **Cancel Subscription** option on the Billing tab. The subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period.

Contact support for Enterprise pricing.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I belong to multiple organizations?">
    Not currently. Each Arkdock account is associated with one organization. If you need access to another organization, you will need a separate account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to data when a member is removed?">
    Evaluations, simulations, agents, and scenarios created by the removed member remain in the organization. Annotations and comments are attributed to their account. The removed member can no longer access the organization.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can a member invite others?">
    Only admins can send invitations. Members can view the team roster but cannot add or remove members.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
