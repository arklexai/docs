## What to include in a support request

Providing the following details helps us respond faster:

- Your **organization name** as it appears in Arkdock
- The **page or feature** where the issue occurs (e.g. "Evaluation detail page", "Start Alignment dialog")
- A **brief description** of what you expected to happen and what actually happened
- Any **error messages** or status codes shown in the UI
- Approximate **time and date** the issue occurred (including your timezone)

## Billing and account questions

For questions about your plan, invoices, or subscription, go to **Settings > Billing** first — the Billing tab shows your current plan, token usage, and a link to manage your subscription through Stripe. See [Settings](/settings) for details.

If you cannot access the Billing tab or need to change the account Owner, contact support@arklex.ai directly.
